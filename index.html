Safety Policy

PLAN DO CHECK ACT

In the planning phase, the organization defines its safety policy and objectives. This involves establishing a clear direction for safety management and identifying the specific goals to be achieved.

The "Do" phase involves the implementation of the planned safety policy. This stage focuses on executing the strategies and action plans developed in the planning phase.

In the checking phase, the organization evaluates the effectiveness of the implemented safety policy.
